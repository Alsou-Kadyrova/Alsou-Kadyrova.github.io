Eden Billnäs offers comfortable homely lodging in a peaceful and beautiful environment by the Mustionjoki river in the village of Billnäs, Raasepori, close to the historical Billnäs Ironworks.

We offer room for lodging in comfortable double rooms in two separate buildings (Eden and Little Eden) throughout the year. Both buildings have kitchens equipped for preparing and serving meals. Linen and bathing towels are included in the room price.

Bathroom facilities are shared. Sauna and an outside barrel bath can be booked based on a separate arrangement prior to arrival. Heating of the barrel bath takes about three four hours, so an advance booking is necessary. Washing machine is available at a separate cost.

The garden and the riverside deck as well as the ever so popular bonfire site are at our guests' disposal during their stay. Coal barbeque facilities are also available in the garden area.

Kayaks, SUP boards and a row boat are available.

There are a total of eight double rooms in two separate buildings (Eden and Little Eden).

Reservations can be made by email (info@edenbillnas.fi) or by phone (+358 40 58 000 82). The reservation starts at 3 pm on the day of arrival, and ends at noon on the day of departure, unless otherwise agreed.

The reservation will be paid in cash or by card (Visa, MasterCard) on arrival for the whole stay.

Pets are admitted based on separate agreement, and an extra cost is charged/animal.

Pricing changes may occur.

Please inform about possible cancellations at least 24 hours prior to the agreed arrival.
